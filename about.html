About me

I am working toward becoming a web developer.

I am a student at UNC Charlotte and I am interested in developing websites that feel organized, readable, and easy to use. I like front-end work because it gives me a direct way to improve the user experience through code, especially when the job is less about starting from scratch and more about seeing what is off, fixing it, and making the final result feel more complete.

The main thing I would want an employer to take from this portfolio is that I am willing to put the work in. I am not the kind of person who wants to just stay in my lane and do the minimum. I want to learn what the team needs, get better at it, and make an effort to understand the people I am working with too.

What I bring to a team

  • I stay with the parts that need cleanup until they behave the way they are supposed to.
  • I pay attention to how pages connect, not just how one isolated screen looks.
  • I can learn new front-end pieces while building, then use that to improve the next version.
  • I am comfortable doing polishing work that makes a project feel more finished.
